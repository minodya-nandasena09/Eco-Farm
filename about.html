About Us

what makes us special

EcoFarm Lanka is a pioneering initiative by the Department of Agriculture (DOA) under the Ministry of Agriculture, aimed at promoting sustainable agriculture and ecological farming practices across Sri Lanka.

We are dedicated to securing the nation's food and nutrition by harnessing advanced agricultural technologies and offering comprehensive services, ensuring a sustainable and prosperous future for all.

Our Mission and Vission

Mission

To foster sustainable agricultural development by disseminating advanced ecological farming practices, ensuring healthy food for today and tomorrow

Vission

To be the leading platform for ecological farming in Sri Lanka, enhancing biodiversity and environmental health through organic and sustainable methods.
